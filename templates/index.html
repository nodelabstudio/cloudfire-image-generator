About Cloudfire

What is this?
Cloudfire turns words into pictures. Type a description, choose an AI model, and the app generates an image — like having an artist who'll draw whatever you describe.

How does it work?
Your prompt goes to either Cloudflare Workers AI or Google Gemini. The model has been trained on millions of images, so it knows how to translate words into pixels. Each generation takes about 10–30 seconds.

The fine print

  • 5 images every 5 minutes per account.
  • Detailed descriptions of people may get blocked by safety filters.
  • The AI can't perfectly control text inside images.
  • Results vary by model — try different ones.
  • Images are saved to your gallery, ready to download.

Features

  • 13 models across Cloudflare and Gemini.
  • Compare mode — race the same prompt across 2–3 models.
  • Prompt history for reusing what worked.
  • Gallery with favorites, tags, and share links.
  • Dashboard with daily and per-model usage.

Which model should I use?
Start with FLUX.1 Schnell (free, great quality). For top results, try FLUX.2 Dev. For Gemini-powered generation, try Gemini 2.5 Flash.
